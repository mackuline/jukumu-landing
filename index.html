What does your CBO need today?

Pick the question that matters most to your group — JUKUMU handles the rest.

01 How do I track M-Pesa payments automatically?

Members pay to your JUKUMU Paybill (4188463) using an account number like E01M04P1R1. JUKUMU reads this code, matches the payment to the right member and project, and records it instantly — no treasurer action needed. The Auto badge confirms it was captured automatically.

02 How do I record cash contributions from members?

Open the Income page in the JUKUMU app, tap Record Cash, select the member, enter the amount and project. Works fully offline — the record is saved to your phone and synced to the server the next time you have internet. A receipt number is generated for your paper records.

03 How do I generate a report for our board or donors?

Go to the Reports page, choose your date range and report type (Board Report, Donor Report, or KRA Compliance), pick English or Kiswahili, then tap Generate. JUKUMU's AI writes a complete narrative report in seconds. You can also download a Word template to fill in manually.

04 How do I manage expenses and stay KRA compliant?

Record each expense with a vendor, amount, and category. JUKUMU automatically calculates 5% Withholding Tax (WHT) on service payments above KES 24,000. Every expense gets a voucher number (e.g. EXP/2025/000012) that cross-references your physical receipts — making KRA audits straightforward.

05 How does JUKUMU work when there's no internet?

JUKUMU is offline-first — all data is saved to your phone first, then synced to the cloud when internet is available. You'll see an offline indicator at the top of the screen. All features (recording income, expenses, viewing members) work normally without internet. Nothing is lost.

06 How do I onboard my CBO onto JUKUMU?

Click Request a Demo and fill in your CBO's details. A JUKUMU team member will contact you within 24 hours to set everything up — including your M-Pesa Paybill, member list, and treasurer account. Setup is handled entirely by JUKUMU staff. No technical skills needed.
